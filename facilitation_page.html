About

Where global programs meet local context.

I am a bilingual training facilitator specializing in leadership and workplace skills development. With experience as a programmer, business analyst, project manager, and product manager, I bring a grounded understanding of real tech and business environments. I focus on people management, helping leaders build strong teams and lead effectively in fast-paced contexts.

I bridge international training content with Vietnamese business culture — ensuring programs are not just translated, but genuinely localized for impact. I partner with global training organizations to deliver engaging, practical sessions grounded in real workplace challenges.
